Mexico Sketch #4

When on vacation, I enjoy brining a small watercolor kit with me. As an alternative to taking pictures, I like to paint watercolors on the spot. The general goal is to capture things quickly and keep things loose. The pieces shown here are from a trip to Playa del Carmen & Tulum, Mexico.
