About Me

Technical Director

I am a talented, motivated technical artist with over 15 years of experience in feature computer animation. I have a strong skill set in look development, asset management and production pipeline debugging & problem-solving. For the past 10 years I have run teams of 20-30 people that produce the digital assets for feature productions at Pixar. This requires strong visual and technical expertise, as well as an ability to manage schedules, budgets, and disparate creative personalities to achieve a high-quality result. I am excited by new technologies and new methods of storytelling. I thrive in collaborative environments, and really enjoy straddling technical and creative disciplines.
